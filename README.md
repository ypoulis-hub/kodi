# Kodi Add-ons

A collection of custom Kodi add-ons for streaming premium content.

---

## Add-ons

### 🎵 YTMusic — YouTube Music for Kodi
Browse and play your YouTube Music Premium library directly in Kodi.

👉 **[View add-on → `ytmusic` branch](../../tree/ytmusic)**

---

### 🏍️ MotoGP VideoPass
Browse and play MotoGP, Moto2, and Moto3 races with your VideoPass subscription.

👉 **[View add-on → `motogp` branch](../../tree/motogp)**
